PIAIC

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing

A privately funded not-for-profit educational initiative started by President of Pakistan, Dr. Arif Alvi, Kazi Rahat Ali, and Zia Khan to promote education, research, and business opportunities in emerging tech fields like:

Cloud Native Computing

This program prepares students to program in open-source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and React Native, introducing them to Microservices and software development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence

The AI program covers the foundations of AI technologies. Students learn open-source languages like Python, Numpy, Pandas, TensorFlow 2.0, and Flask, and they are taught how to deploy practical AI solutions, ending with real-life AI examples.

Blockchain

The Blockchain program emphasizes understanding blockchains like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric) and learning programming languages like Solidity and JavaScript on Node.js frameworks. The program covers Distributed Apps (DApps), Web3, Truffle, and Blockchain-as-a-Service (BaaS).

Internet of Things (IoT)

In this program, students are introduced to open-source technologies such as Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes. They learn to develop Microservices for IoT Edge and IoT Cloud, as well as voice computing for device control.
